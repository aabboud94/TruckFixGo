Manual Test Instructions

  1. Desktop Test: If your screen is ≥768px wide, verify that:
    • Sidebar is visible on the left side
    • No hamburger menu button appears in the header
    • Content is pushed to the right by the sidebar
  2. Mobile Test: Resize browser window to <768px wide, then verify:
    • Sidebar disappears automatically
    • Hamburger menu button appears in the header (top-left)
    • Content fills the full width
  3. Mobile Interaction Test: On mobile view:
    • Click the hamburger menu button
    • Verify sidebar slides in from the left as an overlay
    • Verify a dark backdrop appears behind the sidebar
    • Click the backdrop to close the sidebar
